# Non-Functional Requirements - CampusRide v1.0

## 1. Performance
**NFR-01**: The system shall confirm a ride booking in less than 10 seconds during peak hours 8am-10am.
**NFR-02**: The estimated fare calculation shall complete in less than 3 seconds.

## 2. Reliability
**NFR-03**: The system shall be available 95% of the time during school hours 7am-6pm, Mon-Fri.
**NFR-04**: Driver app shall reconnect automatically if network drops during a ride.

## 3. Usability
**NFR-05**: A new student shall be able to book their first ride in under 3 minutes without training.
**NFR-06**: All buttons and text shall be readable on 5-inch screens.

## 4. Security
**NFR-07**: Student and driver phone numbers shall be masked during chat.
**NFR-08**: All payments and personal data shall be encrypted.

## 5. Scalability
**NFR-09**: The system shall support 500 concurrent students and 100 drivers on FBC campus.
